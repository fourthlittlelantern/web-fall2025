The Fortress

Asghbgahn.

Something is unexpected here. How, though? You are not sure.

The Courtyard.

It welcomes you.

The Great Hall.

Empty. Completely Empty.

The Corridors.

A never-ending maze.

The Kitchen.

A mess.

The Armory.

Suspended in silence.

The Library.

It is full of books, as you would expect a library to be. History books, mostly. But why is that unexpected?

The bookshelves are covered in dust. The chandeliers and lamps shine brightly.

When you open the books, though, not a single one of them is readable. Most books are damaged, ripped, burnt, otherwise destroyed. Some are completely hollowed out inside the cover. Many of the remaining pages are either deliberately blacked out or covered by ink spills. Any remaining text is written in an unfamiliar script.

The... People?

You take a moment to consider this. After careful contemplation, you discover that the population of this fortress is 1, and that is you. There is not a single other person here, only emptiness and silence.

Strange. You could've sworn there was another 0.5 with you.

The Stairs.

A narrow spiral staircase, winding up into the tower, disappearing into darkness. It awaits you.

Perhaps it is time to go.

Go? Go onwards, of course, like you have been this entire journey.
