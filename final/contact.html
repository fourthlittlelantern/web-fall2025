About Me

Me? You want to know about me?

Your tour guide.

I am the official tour guide of Asghbgahn, a title recognized even by other kingdoms. I was appointed many, many cycles ago, by the first king of Asghbgahn himself. Since then, I have guided countless travelers like you through these lands.

A signpost?

I am not a signpost.

No, I am not a signpost. Last cycle, for example, I was a crow. Throughout the cycles, I have also been a ant, a scroll, a voice, a monocole, a child, and many more.


A photograph, captured 73 years ago by a curious traveler carrying a camera. Her bones now rest buried on the shores of The Lake.
A portrait, painted 7 cycles ago by a friendly traveler who was a also a talented artist. He settled down and lived a wonderful, full life in The City.
A sketch from the notebook of a clever young man who I met 4 cycles ago. It's a pity what happened to him. He had too many ideas, too much intelligence for his own good.

In the end, I am merely a tour guide.

Contact

Find me anytime in The Foothills, at the border of Asghbgahn and the Interlands, or send a letter addressed directly to "The Tour Guide." The messenger will know where to find me.
